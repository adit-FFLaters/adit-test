👋 About Alex

Alex is the co-founder and CEO of Laters.com (formerly Fly Fairly), the buy-now-pay-later travel platform he started in 2024 to fix the part of flight booking the industry never touched: how you actually pay. Before Laters, he led commercial and partnership teams at Agoda, Booking.com, eBay, and ShopBack, and spent time at IWG plc — giving him a front-row view of e-commerce, OTA economics, and BNPL from three continents.

He holds a business degree from Nottingham Trent University and completed executive education in Business & Leadership Principles at Harvard Business School. Under his leadership, Laters.com reached profitability, expanded to 10+ markets, and won the 2025 FinoPitch Audience Award.
